I'm Nicholas Wile,

A Computer Science graduate from Kennesaw State University.

I have designed, developed, and deployed many types of software throughout my college years. From video games using C# with Unity '19-'21 and C++ with Unreal 4 & 5 to website front-ends using HTML5 and CSS3; from desktop applications for PC and Mac to mobile apps for Android platforms. Beyond software engineering I have also explored many other fields within computer science, especially computer graphics, where I have extensive experience in rendering, modeling, and animation using Autodesk Maya '19 & '22. I have assembled a Portfolio highlighting some of my favorite projects I've worked on over the past few years, having selected the ones that I believe best illustrate how my skills and processes have developed over time.
